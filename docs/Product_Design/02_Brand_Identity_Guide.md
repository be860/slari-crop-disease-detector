# Document Purpose

This Brand Identity Guide defines the visual and verbal identity of SLARI CropCare. It establishes the standards for the application's name, logo, typography, colors, iconography, imagery, and communication style to ensure consistency across the mobile application, documentation, presentations, and future releases.

This document serves as the single source of truth for all branding decisions throughout the project lifecycle.

# 1. Brand Overview

SLARI CropCare is a mobile platform designed specifically for Sierra Leone's agricultural sector. The brand combines modern technology with local agricultural realities to provide farmers with a trusted, practical, and easy-to-use crop disease detection solution.

Rather than emphasizing Artificial Intelligence, the brand focuses on helping farmers solve real agricultural problems quickly and confidently.

The identity should feel approachable, trustworthy, and rooted in the local farming context.

---

# 2. Brand Vision

To become Sierra Leone's most trusted digital platform for crop health management and agricultural advisory services.

---

# 3. Brand Mission

To empower farmers, Agricultural Extension Officers, and researchers with an intelligent mobile platform that delivers accessible crop disease detection and practical advice, helping improve productivity and strengthen food security across Sierra Leone.

---

# 4. Brand Values

The SLARI CropCare brand is built on six core values.

### Trust

Users should feel confident that the application provides reliable and helpful information.

### Simplicity

The application should remain easy to understand regardless of the user's digital literacy.

### Accessibility

Technology should be available to everyone, including users in rural communities with limited connectivity.

### Innovation

Artificial Intelligence should enhance agricultural practices without making the application complicated.

### Local Relevance

Every aspect of the application should reflect Sierra Leone's farming environment, crops, and user needs.

### Sustainability

The platform should encourage responsible farming practices through disease prevention and treatment guidance.

---

# 5. Brand Personality

The brand should consistently communicate the following characteristics.

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Friendly | Welcoming and easy to use |
| Trustworthy | Accurate, dependable, and honest |
| Practical | Focused on solving real farming problems |
| Modern | Uses current technology without feeling overly technical |
| Local | Designed specifically for Sierra Leone |
| Professional | Suitable for research institutions and agricultural experts |

---

# 6. Product Name

## Official Name

**SLARI CropCare**

The product name combines the trusted identity of the Sierra Leone Agricultural Research Institute (SLARI) with the platform's primary purpose of supporting crop health management.

---

# 7. Official Tagline

## Scan. Detect. Treat.

The tagline summarizes the complete user journey.

- Scan an infected crop leaf.
- Detect the likely disease.
- Receive practical advice and treatment recommendations.

It should appear consistently across splash screens, presentations, documentation, and promotional materials.

---

# 8. Brand Voice

Communication throughout the application should use clear, concise, and simple English.

## Preferred Language

✔ Scan Leaf

✔ Detect Disease

✔ View Advice

✔ My Scans

✔ Sign In

✔ Continue as Guest

✔ Disease Detected

✔ Confidence

✔ Recommended Next Step

---

## Avoid Technical Language

✘ Run AI Inference

✘ Image Classification

✘ Execute Detection Pipeline

✘ Prediction Probability Matrix

✘ TensorFlow Processing

The technology should remain invisible to the user.

---

# 9. Visual Identity

The visual identity should communicate agriculture, trust, simplicity, and modern technology without appearing overly technical.

The interface should feel clean, spacious, and easy to navigate.

Rounded corners, generous spacing, and soft colors should create a calm and welcoming experience.

---

# 10. Logo System

## Logo Concept

The logo represents the relationship between agriculture, crop protection, and intelligent decision-making.

The proposed concept consists of:

- A clean leaf silhouette representing healthy crops.
- A subtle magnifying glass integrated into the leaf to symbolize disease inspection.
- Four internal leaf veins representing the four supported crops:
  - Cassava
  - Groundnut
  - Soybean
  - Cowpea
- A circular enclosing shape representing protection, sustainability, and continuous agricultural support.

The logo should remain simple enough to be recognizable at small sizes.

---

## Logo Variations

The brand should include:

- Primary Logo
- Horizontal Logo
- App Icon
- Monochrome Logo
- White Logo for dark backgrounds

# 13. Iconography

The application will use a single consistent icon family.

Recommended Library:

**Phosphor Icons**

Icons should use:

- Rounded style
- Medium stroke weight
- Simple shapes
- Consistent sizing

---

# 14. Imagery Style

Photography should prioritize:

- Real Sierra Leonean farms
- Cassava fields
- Groundnut cultivation
- Soybean farms
- Cowpea fields
- Healthy crop leaves
- Farmers interacting with crops

Avoid generic stock photography that does not represent local agriculture.

---

# 15. UI Design Principles

The interface should follow these principles.

### Large touch targets

Buttons should be easy to tap on smaller Android devices.

---

### Minimal cognitive load

Present only the information users need at each step.

---

### Progressive disclosure

Display the Smart Diagnosis Card first, followed by detailed advice.

---

### Offline First

Users should never feel blocked because of poor internet connectivity.

---

### Consistency

Colors, spacing, typography, and interactions should remain uniform throughout the application.

---

# 16. Accessibility

The application should support:

- High color contrast
- Readable font sizes
- Clear labels
- Large buttons
- Simple navigation
- Descriptive icons

---

# 17. Brand Dos and Don'ts

## Do

✔ Keep messaging simple.

✔ Focus on solving farming problems.

✔ Use authentic agricultural imagery.

✔ Maintain consistent spacing and typography.

✔ Prioritize clarity over decoration.

---

## Don't

✘ Overuse AI-related terminology.

✘ Use unrelated farming imagery.

✘ Mix multiple font families.

✘ Overcomplicate the interface.

✘ Depend on internet connectivity for core features.

---

# 18. Future Brand Evolution

As the platform grows, the brand may expand to include:

- Additional Sierra Leonean crops.
- Multilingual support.
- Voice-based interactions.
- Expert consultation services.
- Research dashboards.
- Regional agricultural partnerships.

The core identity of trust, simplicity, and local relevance should remain unchanged.
